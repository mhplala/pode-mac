WHY WE MADE THIS

Most podcast apps want to keep you. They surface what you might play, count your minutes, and lose the thread of what you actually heard. Pode goes the other way. It hands you back the transcript, lets you mark what stayed with you, and otherwise gets out of the way. The interface is paper-warm and quiet for a reason — so the voice can come through.

EVERY WORD, KEPT

Every word, in writing.

The best line in a three-hour conversation isn't always where you'd guess. Pode transcribes every episode — on your Mac, or through your own AI provider — so the moments you want to come back to stay searchable, quotable, and yours to revisit.

A QUIETER LIBRARY

The lines that stay.
